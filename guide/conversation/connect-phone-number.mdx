Connecting a phone number to the platform is a crucial step to enable messaging and business interactions via WhatsApp.
This guide explains the available methods for integrating your phone number, whether you want to use a number provided by Cogfy, bring your own existing number, or connect a WhatsApp Business App number for coexistence.
Choose the option that best fits your needs and follow the instructions to complete the setup process.

> To connect your phone number, visit [messenger.cogfy.com/:WORKSPACE/new-phone-number](https://messenger.cogfy.com/:WORKSPACE/new-phone-number).

## 1. Get a Phone Number with Cogfy

Use a phone number provided by Cogfy.
- Select your desired area code and set a display name for your new number.
- Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the setup.

## 2. Bring Your Own Phone Number

Use an existing phone number with Cogfy.
- Connect your own number by following the guided steps.
- Verify your phone number and link it to a WhatsApp Business Account (WABA).

## 3. Connect WhatsApp Business App Number (Coexistence)

Integrate an existing WhatsApp Business App number with Cogfy.
- Connect your Facebook account.
- Select your business portfolio.
- Follow the instructions to onboard your WhatsApp Business App for coexistence with Cogfy.

Each method provides a step-by-step flow to ensure a smooth connection process.
Choose the option that best fits your needs and follow the instructions provided in the interface.

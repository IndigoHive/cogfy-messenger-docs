Analytics in Cogfy Messenger provide insights into your workspace's performance, user interactions, and assistant effectiveness. By leveraging these analytics, you can make informed decisions to optimize your assistants and enhance user experience.

> To monitor assistant performance, metrics, and user interactions, visit [messenger.cogfy.com/:WORKSPACE/analytics](https://messenger.cogfy.com/:WORKSPACE/analytics).
